Food Guide

Here are the top 4 popular resturant destination in Niagara fall, click on the image to book to make a reservation.

Skylon Tower

Experience dining at 775 feet above the magnificent Niagara Falls in the world-famous Revolving Dining Room. Enjoy award-winning continental cuisine as you take in the breathtaking 360-degree views of the falls and the surrounding cityscape.

Located at: 5200 Robinson St, Niagara Falls, ON L2G 2A2, Canada

Peller Estates The Winery Restaurant

Nestled in the heart of Niagara-on-the-Lake, this restaurant offers a sophisticated farm-to-table dining experience with sweeping vineyard views. Indulge in a menu crafted by a celebrated chef, with each dish perfectly paired with one of the estate's award-winning wines.

Located at: 290 John St E, Niagara-on-the-Lake, ON L0S 1J0, Canada

Table rock house

Located just steps from the brink of the Canadian Horseshoe Falls, this restaurant offers the closest possible dining view of the natural wonder. With a menu that is locally sourced and internationally inspired, you can enjoy a spectacular meal against the backdrop of the thundering water.

Located at: 6650 Niagara River Pkwy, Niagara Falls, ON L2E 6T2, Canada

Fallsview Grand Buffet

Situated in the Sheraton Fallsview Hotel, this buffet provides stunning panoramic views of Niagara Falls. It features an impressive 80-foot spread of diverse and delicious dishes, complete with live-action cooking stations for a dynamic dining experience.

Located at: 5875 Falls Ave, Niagara Falls, ON L2G 3K7, Canada
