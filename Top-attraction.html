Niagara Falls

Top Attractions

Journey Behind the Falls

Stand in the heart of Niagara! Descend 125 feet and explore 130-year-old tunnels that lead to observation decks directly behind the massive sheet of the Horseshoe Falls.

Located at: 6650 Niagara River Pkwy, Niagara Falls, ON

Skylon Tower

Get a breathtaking 360-degree view from 775 feet high. The observation deck provides the best aerial perspective of the Falls, the Great Gorge, and the city skylines.

Located at: 5200 Robinson St, Niagara Falls, ON

Niagara SkyWheel

Soar 175 feet above the horizon for a spectacular panoramic view of the Falls. Ride in climate-controlled gondolas for a comfortable experience day or night, year-round.

Located at: 4960 Clifton Hill, Niagara Falls, ON

Niagara City Cruises

Embark on an iconic boat tour that takes you into the very heart of the thundering and misty basin of the Canadian Horseshoe Falls. Provided with a recyclable poncho, you will feel the awesome power of the water on this unforgettable 20-minute voyage. This quintessential Niagara experience offers a uniquely intimate perspective right from the water's surface.

Located at: 5920 Niagara River Pkwy, Niagara Falls, ON

White Water Walk

Descend into the Niagara Gorge by elevator to stroll along a scenic boardwalk at the very edge of the river's ferocious Class 6 rapids. This self-guided tour allows you to safely witness the incredible speed and power of one of the world's most turbulent stretches of whitewater. It's an awe-inspiring experience that showcases a different, wilder side of the Niagara River's might.

Located at: 4330 River Rd, Niagara Falls, ON
